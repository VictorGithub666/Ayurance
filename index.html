Our Treatments

Panchkarma Therapies

Seven specialised treatments, each targeting specific dosha imbalances and chronic conditions — designed for deep healing.

Main Therapy

Vamana

Controlled, physician-supervised therapeutic emesis to expel excess Kapha dosha and accumulated toxins from the stomach and respiratory tract.

Therapeutic Vomiting · Kapha
Main Therapy

Virechana

Classical Ayurvedic controlled therapeutic purgation to eliminate excess Pitta dosha and flush accumulated toxins through the lower GI tract.

Therapeutic Diarrhoea · Pitta
Main Therapy

Vasti (Basti)

Introduction of medicated oils or herbal decoctions into the colon through the rectum. Balances Vata and nourishes deep tissues.

Therapeutic Enema · Vata · Colon
Main Therapy

Raktamoksha

Classical bloodletting and purification therapy to eliminate vitiated blood and toxins, particularly driven by aggravated Pitta dosha.

Therapeutic Blood Letting · Pitta
Main Therapy

Nasya

Administration of medicinal oils, herbal juices, or powders into the nasal passages. Cleanses and nourishes the head and neck.

Therapeutic Brain Cleansing · Sinus
Supportive

Janu Vasti

Warm medicated herbal oil retained over the knee joint inside a black gram dough dam. Highly effective for knee pain, stiffness, and osteoarthritis.

Knees · Joints · Arthritis
Supportive

Kati Vasti

Warm medicated oil pooled over the lower back within a dough dam. Relieves chronic back pain, sciatica, lumbar spondylosis, and muscle spasms.

Back · Sciatica · Spine
Supportive

Abhyanga

Rhythmic, full-body warm herbal oil massage. Loosens deep-seated toxins, lubricates joints, and pacifies the nervous system.

Full Body · Lymphatic · Dosha
